📌 Key Takeaways

  • 1. Exceptional Model Performance
    ROC-AUC of 1.0000 and F1-macro of 0.9985 indicate near-perfect multi-class classification with zero false negatives on critical High Risk class.
  • 2. Interpretability via SHAP
    SHAP analysis confirms credit constraint as the dominant distress predictor, aligning with World Bank SME financing gap literature and enabling transparent decision-making.
  • 3. Algorithm Selection Matters
    XGBoost's gradient boosting with regularisation outperforms both linear models (F1-macro 0.78) and unregularised Random Forests, highlighting the importance of algorithm selection for imbalanced classification.
  • 4. Ready for Deployment
    The tuned model's exceptional test set performance, combined with SHAP interpretability, makes it suitable for responsible AI deployment in high-stakes SME credit and policy contexts.
